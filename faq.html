Program

1) What is admission procedure and what are eligibility criteria to get admission?

Admission procedure:

  • Admissions for the technical courses are carried out by admission regulating authority of Government of Maharashtra.
  • 80% quota of the intake of each branch will be filled by admission regulating authority through Centralized Admission Process( CAP)
  • For CAP admissions students have to follow the guidelines and procedures given on DTE website.
  • For the detail information about admissions 2020 visit please visit MHT-CET 2019 website.
  • 20% seats are reserved for Institute Level quota.
  • For institute level quota students shall contact “Admission Cell” of our institute.
  • We request you to visit our college on any working day between 9:30 AM to 5:30 PM.

2) What is the placement scenario in your Institute?

  • We have well established state of the art Training and Placement cell equipped with competent professionals.
  • Efficient academic planning along with various add on courses such as Aptitude Training , JLPT, Analytical Capabilities, we make our students industry ready.
  • Training and Placement cell trains the students professionally for soft skills, communication skills, aptitude, group discussion & personal interview and technical courses to help them get placed in industry.
  • Strong placement is the USP of our institute.
